Most of the TVs will be broken, which will make your job trickier. But you can still learn from the antennas on broken TVs. Everyone in the town has been adjusting their TV to pick up either one channel or the other. This means that you will be seeing two distinct "types" of antennas.

Furthermore, since CH1 and CH2 are the only channels, you can be confident that every TV antenna you see, broken or otherwise, was set that way with the intention of tuning in one of those two stations.
