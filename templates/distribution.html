Most of the TVs brought into your shop will be broken (indicated by the broken glass), which will make your job trickier. Broken TVs look like this:

While it might at first seem like you can't learn anything from a broken TV, in fact, you can still learn from these items. For example, by closely examining different TVs you might pick up on the "natural groupings" of antennas. The reason for the grouping is that everyone in this town has adjusted their TV to pick up either CH1 or CH2 and these involve different antenna settings. There are two "types" of antenna settings that you could discover just by watching. Keep in mind, however, that there may be small variations in how any individual TV is set based on the reception at the person's home.

Since CH1 and CH2 are the only channels in this pretend town, you can be confident that every TV antenna you see, broken or working, was set that way with the intention of picking up one of the two channels. The antennas on the TVs are never broken... just the glass and electronics inside is broken on some of the TVs.
