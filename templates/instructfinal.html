In summary, these are the basics of the task:

  • All the TVs in the pretend town are tuned to CH1 or CH2.
  • People tune their TVs to pick up the two channels by adjusting:
    • The length of the bar and
    • Its angle.
  • This means there will be two different "types" of antennas corresponding to the two channels.
  • Most TVs will be broken, but you can learn from both the broken and working TVs if you pay attention.

The game has two phases. First, you will see many examples of TVs that are brought into the shop. After viewing each, click continue to move on to the next one. Try to figure out what might make a TV pick up CH1 or CH2. Afterwards, you will be shown a set of new TVs all with broken screens and will be asked to guess which channel they pick up.

On the next screen, you will be asked to complete a brief quiz to make sure you have understood the instructions.
