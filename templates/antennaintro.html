To pick up their favorite channel, the residents adjust simple antennas, made out of a single bar. The antennas look like this:

The antennas can be adjusted in only two ways: by extending or collapsing the length or by swiveling the angle.

Your job in this task will be to learn which antenna settings pick up CH1 and CH2.

When performing the task, please try not to bring too many assumptions about how your antenna at home might work. This is meant to be a simple video game where things work a little bit different than in the real world.
